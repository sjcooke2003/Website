Overview

Counsel Built for Business

Whether you are launching a startup, restructuring an existing enterprise, or navigating a complex transaction, Gary E. Cooke II brings over three decades of Illinois business law experience to bear on your behalf.

Mr. Cooke works closely with business owners, executives, and entrepreneurs to structure their affairs for maximum protection and long-term success. His approach combines rigorous legal analysis with practical, real-world guidance.

"The best legal counsel doesn't just solve today's problem — it helps you anticipate and prevent tomorrow's."

From the earliest stages of entity formation through growth, partnership agreements, and eventual succession or sale, Cooke's Law is your trusted legal partner throughout the full lifecycle of your business.

Services Include
  • Business entity formation (LLC, Corporation, Partnership)
  • Operating agreements and bylaws drafting
  • Contract drafting, review, and negotiation
  • Mergers, acquisitions, and business sales
  • Shareholder and partnership agreements
  • Employment agreements and non-competes
  • Regulatory compliance and risk management
  • Business succession planning
  • Dispute resolution and transactional counsel
Why It Matters

Protecting Your Investment

Sound legal structure is the foundation of every successful business. Mr. Cooke helps you build it right from the start.

Proper entity structure and contractual safeguards protect your personal assets and limit liability exposure in the event of disputes or claims.

Clear Agreements

Well-drafted agreements between partners, employees, and vendors prevent misunderstandings and provide a clear framework for resolving any disputes that arise.

Growth Ready

Thoughtful legal architecture scales with your business — from seed stage through growth, fundraising, and eventual exit or succession.
