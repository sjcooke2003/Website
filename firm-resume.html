Firm Résumé

The professional background, credentials, and career history of Gary E. Cooke II — Attorney at Law, licensed in Illinois since 1988.

Professional Profile

Gary E. Cooke II

Background

Gary E. Cooke II is an attorney based in the Chicago metropolitan area who has been in continuous practice since 1988. Over a career spanning more than three decades, Mr. Cooke has developed deep expertise across business law, finance, real estate, and life and estate planning.

Mr. Cooke is licensed to practice law in the State of Illinois. He has maintained offices in Chicago's most prestigious business addresses throughout his career, most recently relocating to Tinley Park, Illinois in 2023.

Philosophy

Mr. Cooke founded Cooke's Law with a clear mission: to bring the kind of sophisticated, technology-forward legal counsel typically associated with large firms to individual clients and small-to-medium businesses on a personal, accessible basis.

He believes that every client deserves to fully understand their legal situation, and invests significant time ensuring clients are informed partners in their own legal matters — not passive recipients of advice they don't understand.

Technology & Innovation

Throughout his career, Mr. Cooke has been an early and enthusiastic adopter of technology in legal practice. From early internet-based client services to the use of webcasts and online document delivery, Cooke's Law has consistently embraced technology as a means of providing better, more accessible legal services.
