About

Three Decades of
Trusted Legal Service

Welcome to the internet home of Gary E. Cooke II and to Cooke's Law. Mr. Cooke is an attorney in Chicago where he has been practicing law since 1988, bringing over three decades of experience to every client engagement.

Cooke's Law was created to complement Mr. Cooke's services and to enable you to take advantage of his experience, use of technology, and meticulous organization. Throughout Cooke's Law you will find information about Mr. Cooke, possible answers to questions you may have, general legal discussions, webcasts, forms, and links to additional information.

Mr. Cooke is licensed to practice law in Illinois. He is committed to bringing personal attention and professional excellence to every matter — whether you're a business owner, investor, or individual seeking sound legal guidance.
